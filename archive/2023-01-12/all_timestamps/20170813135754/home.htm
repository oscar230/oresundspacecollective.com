March 12th

Well, I think all the OSC fans out there are going to be blown away by the music we made this weekend! We recorded 6 hrs and 53 minutes of jams to 24 channels. The tape was running so to speak when nothing was happening sometimes, so in the end we probably only have about 6 hrs or so of jams, only. The sound is totally amazing and have the PA set up in the recording room worked so much better for most everyone and we really played some killer hypnotic grooves and the sound is mind blowing... We have enough material to make at least a double CD, perhaps more.. We will spend the next few weeks digesting this stuff and trying to figure out what pieces we should spend the time to mix properly. We will keep you posted and probably put up a few teasers for you as well.. Another CD review has just been posted on the web so check it out at Melo's Close to the Edge site!

Nov 18th, 2005

We are extremely happy to announce that the Øresund Space Collective debut CD will be released on the great Malmö,
Sweden based record label, Transubstans in early January. It will be a hand numbered limited edition of 550 copies.

To celebrate this we will have a CD release party at Dragens Hule on January 21st. The tracks on the CD are as follows: Faked it all the Way,
Consumed by the Goblin, OSC Bolero, Grab a Cab, Falling Stardrops, Moonhead and Sunrise.

It is about 73 minutes of music ambient-cosmic krautrock influenced space rock.

/ Scott and the OSC
