Welcome to our Sessions page. Here you will find hours and hours of our free form space rock jams. As there is so much music here one might ask, where to start? I have decided to try to categorize some of the jams below for you into different styles. Some titles are obvious (Funky, reggae, Jazzy) so these will not be placed in a catagory. In addition, Michael, our distiguished bass player has selected some jams from most sessions that are a good starting point to jump into the world of OSC. These are denoted by the .

Today, we have added an easy to use Paypal donation logo you can just click on below and donate to our cause. Just $1, £1 or 1€ everytime you come and download a new session or whatever you feel like is cool with us. Some people have more money than others. We just hope you can support us a tiny bit while we continue to give you all the hours and hours of free music. All we ask is you try to buy our studio CDs and donate a little, when you can. No big deal. In return, you will get the music in a higher quality sound. In addition, we will put everyones name who donates in our CD liner notes! I hope that is cool with you guys and gals out in space land...
