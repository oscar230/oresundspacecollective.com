Live in Tampere 2018

Posted onMarch 13, 2019March 13, 2019AuthorDr SpaceLeave a comment

This was the last show of our 4 show mini-tour of Sweden and Finland in Oct 2018. The band was on fire from the start with a killer high energy set.This was released and given away to our subscribers in March 2019 but a limited number are to be sold on bandcamp and at shows in May-June 2019.

Credits

Released March 22nd, 2019. 100 copies.
Jiri- Bass, Tim- Drums, Jonathan- Guitar, Violin, Vemund- Guitar, Dr Space- Synths, speaking, Mogens- synths
Recorded by the local soundman on 9 channels. Mixed in Logic with UAD apollo platform by Jonathan Segel. 
Artwork by David Graham. Photos by Sabine.

https://oresundspacecollective.bandcamp.com/album/live-in-tampere-2018
