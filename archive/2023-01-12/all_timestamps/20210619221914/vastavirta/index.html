Vastavirta

Posted onOctober 13, 2020October 13, 2020AuthorDr Space

Catalog Nr: ØSC2020-LS004
Release Date: Oct 2020

Warped Transmission (edit) 13:17 
Centre of the Universe 21:28
Psychospaceabilly 22:11 
The End of the Space Race 22:47:41

In May-June 2019, ØSC undertook our most ambitious tour going around the Baltic Sea starting in Copenhagen and ending in Stockholm.  The band played a lot of amazing and adventurous shows with a lot of different guests but this was one of the most unique shows with Donald Lupo on banjo and Vesa Partti on guitar synth, guitar and synthesizer (only a little bit).  This was the 2nd to last show on the tour and although the crowd was not large (perhaps 40 people), we were inspired still. What a great show…

The band on this night was: Jiri- Bass, Tim- Drums, Dr Space- Synths, Vesa- Synths, Synth Guitar, Guitar, Jonathan- Violin and Guitar, Donald- Banjo (Tracks 3,4), Vemund- Guitar (tracks 1-3)-

Recorded by the local soundman and mixed by Jonathan Segel in Stockholm, Sweden 2019. Edited by Dr Space.
Note: The opening number is missing the first 8 minutes in order to fit most of the concert onto a single CD.

Artwork by David Graham.
