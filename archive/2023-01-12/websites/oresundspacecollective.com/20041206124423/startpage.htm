The Øresund Space Collective is a group of like minded musicians
 who get together every 2-3 weeks for free form space rock jam
sessions in Malmö and Copenhagen. The line up of each session is
different and features members of such bands as Mantric Muse,
Bland Bladen, Carpet Knights, and Gas Giant. All the sessions are
recorded and soon samples will be made available to the public.
The long term goal of the project is to create interesting and
unique music and to eventually go out and play live concerts and
tours as well. I hope that we will also release some CD's or CD-rs
to the public as well and do some multitrack recording.
