In August, we will release our 3rd CD as a deluxe digipack, limited to 1000 copies. It is will be released and distributed via Transubstans Records as have all our releases. It features Rumble (38mins), The Black Tomato (32mins) and Viking Cleaner (6 mins) for a total of 77 minutes. I think you will find that these are some of our coolest journeys in sound…. I know you will like them. The sound production is the best we have ever had (and I thought our other CDs had amazing sound!). Thanks to Henrik Udd for the amazing mix and Hans for the mastering job!
