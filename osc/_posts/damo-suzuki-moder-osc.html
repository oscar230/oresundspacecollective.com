Released in 2013 on Space Rock Productions. SRP020 --- ### About the Album: This album is a collaboration between Øresund Space Collective and Damo Suzuki. The music captures a mixture of intense energy and psychedelic improvisation. It was recorded in a limited number of deluxe sleeves, with colored vinyl editions being a rare collectible. The release showcases the synergy between the musicians, especially in the tracks capturing moments of pure improvisation. --- Released: 2013 Label: Space Rock Productions Catalog: SRP020 Format: Deluxe Sleeve, 500 numbered copies - 100 Colored Vinyl - 400 Black Vinyl
