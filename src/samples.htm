Øresund Space Jams 2004-5

Here you will find the full jams from our sessions. These are encoded as high quality MP3 files, so they are quite big. If you are interested in in hearing the samples but only have a modem, please send me email and I will put up some lower quality samples. Enjoy! We plan to release some of these sessions as limited edition CDs, similar to the Quarkspace Spacefold series.

Special Note: All of the samples on the site have recently had compression and reverb added in addition to new EQ on some of the tracks to improve the sound. So if you had downloaded the samples prior to 2/27/05, then you may hear some differences in the sound on the new MP3s for Jam sessions 1-6.
